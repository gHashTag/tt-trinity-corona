## Research (this loop, web available)

Completing the count guard is the "no orphan literals" corollary of the
single-source discipline: a number stated in prose should either be generated or
checked against its source, never both free-floating and authoritative. The two
on-die counts are derived from the same CATALOG flags the rest of the suite reads,
so they now move in lockstep with the design. This is the same lesson as the
gate-count and formal-task fixes -- close the copy, keep the check.
