## Scientific Research

### AetherFloat Format Family (New)

arXiv:2603.08741 (February 2026). Block-Scale-Free quad-radix (base-4) floating-point for AI accelerators. AF8 eliminates dynamic block-scaling (AMAX) hardware: 33% area, 22% power reduction vs IEEE 754 MAC units. AF16 positioned as BF16 replacement. Directly relevant as a new format family for Corona's catalog.

### FLoPS: P3109 Formal Verification in Lean

arXiv:2602.15965 (February 2026, revised May 2026). Comprehensive formal model of IEEE P3109 minifloats in Lean theorem prover. Discovers novel FastTwoSum properties under saturation semantics. Potential cross-validation target for Corona's ROM tables.
