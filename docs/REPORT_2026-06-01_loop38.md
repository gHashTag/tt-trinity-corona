### 4. Research Findings

**SymbiYosys autotune**: `sby --autotune` benchmarks all solvers per design. For sub-byte LUTs, `smtbmc bitwuzla` consistently outperforms z3.

**Bitwuzla replaces Boolector**: Recommended as the new default bit-vector SMT solver in the YosysHQ ecosystem. Our .sby files still use z3 — this works but bitwuzla may be faster in CI.

**Cover as completeness canary**: If any cover property fails, the assumptions are over-constrained and passing asserts may be vacuously true. This is the standard technique to prevent "proofs that prove nothing."

**No TT formal precedent found**: This project appears to be the most formally-verified Tiny Tapeout design to date (19 .sby configs, 80-entry exhaustive ROM golden LUT, 256-entry posit8 LUT).
