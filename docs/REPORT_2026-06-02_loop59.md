## Scientific Research

### Post-Silicon Testing (Major Finding)

**microcotb** — Lightweight cocotb variant running on microcontrollers for hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) testing of TT designs. The TT demoboard RP2350 runs microcotb testbenches directly against the ASIC via MicroPython SDK. Corona's post-silicon ROM readback tests could be ported from cocotb simulation to microcotb on the demoboard.

**FabricFox FPGA Breakout** (Latch-Up 2026) — Lattice iCE40 UP5K board pin-compatible with TT demoboard for pre-silicon FPGA validation.
