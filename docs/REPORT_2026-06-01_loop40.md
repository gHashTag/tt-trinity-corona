### 2. Research Findings

**IEEE P3109 status (Jun 2026):** Interim Report v3.0 (Aug 2025) introduced systematic `binaryKpP` naming. Exponent bias reversed back to 2^(w-1) in Jul 2025. Still unratified — our catalog aligns with interim naming but should flag as draft-compliant.

**FLoPS (Feb 2026):** Machine-checked Lean formalization of P3109 minifloat family. Directly usable as golden reference for test vector generation — could supplement our SymbiYosys formal proofs with independent specification checking.

**MX v1.0 still current:** No OCP MX v2 published. NVIDIA NVFP4 (dual-level FP8+FP32 scaling) and MX+ (near-FP8 quality at 4.25 bits/element) are vendor extensions. Our MX format catalog covers the canonical set.

**Posit silicon efficiency:** May 2025 paper shows 47.9% LUT reduction for posit codecs in RISC-V — validates that our posit8 decoder is silicon-efficient at this technology node.

**SymbiYosys RTL equivalence:** StarVLSI (May 2026) documents a complete open-source RTL equivalence checking flow with SymbiYosys — viable for pre-vs-post-synthesis equivalence if needed.
