### GF180MCU Timing at 50 MHz
No published benchmark confirms 50 MHz on GF180MCU specifically. SKY130 (130nm)
designs reach 50-98 MHz. GF180MCU (180nm) will be slower. **50 MHz is plausible
but not guaranteed — plan for 25-40 MHz as safe target.** PDK constrains max
transition to 20% of clock period (4ns at 50 MHz). Our combinational decoders
have short critical paths; the ROM case statement is the longest path.

### Post-Silicon Debug for TT
Designers receive assembled dev boards. Testing is community-driven via Discord.
Expect slower-than-simulated clocks due to scan chain through 200+ designs.
Some designers build JTAG TAP debug infrastructure into their TT tiles.
