## Research: FP8 E4M3 Variant Landscape

| Variant | Bias | NaN | Inf | Subnormals | Max |
|---------|------|-----|-----|------------|-----|
| OCP MX E4M3 | 7 | 0x7F, 0xFF | None | Yes | ±448 |
| Standard E4M3 (H100/TE) | 7 | 0x7F, 0xFF | None | Yes | ±448 |
| E4M3 FNUZ | **8** | **0x80 only** | None | **No** | ±240 |

Key finding: OCP MX E4M3 and NVIDIA/TE E4M3FN are **identical encodings**.
"MX" refers to the block scaling wrapper, not a different element format.
FNUZ (fmt_id=69) is genuinely different but not FLAG_ON_DIE.
