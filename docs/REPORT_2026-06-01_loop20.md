## Research Summary (Loop 20)

### INT4 for LLMs — No Standard, One Encoding
GPTQ, AWQ, SqueezeLLM all use signed two's complement INT4 [-8,+7] with
group-wise scaling (groups of 128). MXINT4 does not exist in OCP MX v1.0.
Our INT4 decoder covers the dominant wire format.

### GF180MCU Gotchas
- PDK is alpha/experimental — known DRC pitfalls with diffusion/tap abutting
- Metal 5 reserved by TT power grid (we don't control routing)
- Max frequency ~40-80 MHz at 180nm; 50 MHz target is reasonable
- LibreLane 3.0 (Mar 2026) is the recommended flow for GF180MCU

### Format-Aware Compression
ZipNN extended to FP8/FP4 (Intel, Aug 2025): lossless compression by
independently entropy-coding exponent vs mantissa fields. Our format oracle
could tag which compression scheme applies to each format — future ROM
metadata extension.

### Open-Source EDA Updates
LibreLane 3.0 (FOSSi Foundation) replaced OpenLane for GF180MCU. OpenROAD
enabled 600+ tapeouts. wafer.space GF180MCU Run 2 announced May 2026.
