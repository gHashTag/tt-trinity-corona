# ADR-0002: Target Shuttle TTGF26a

## Status

Accepted (2026-06-01)

## Context

The original plan targeted TTGF26b (expected 2026 Q4). TTGF26a is currently open
for submissions (2026-04-17 to 2026-06-22), with estimated chip delivery
2026-10-01 to 2026-11-15.

## Decision

Target TTGF26a instead of TTGF26b. This provides an earlier submission window
and faster silicon feedback, at the cost of a compressed development timeline.

## Consequences

- Submission deadline: 2026-06-22 (TTGF26a close)
- Silicon delivery: ~2026-10 to 2026-11
- Development timeline compressed from 45-55 days to ~21 days
- MVP-5 fallback (ROM + 5 converters) becomes the likely deliverable
- Phase A must complete within 1 week; Phase B within 1 week; Phase C (reduced)
  within 1 week
