### 3. Research Findings

**EQY (YosysHQ):** RTL-vs-netlist formal equivalence checker, usable but not production-certified. Supplements but doesn't replace GLS. A Cornell 2025 paper notes EQY bugs can manifest as missed design bugs.

**TTGF26a submission process:** No standalone checklist. Submit via app.tinytapeout.com. Must pass GDS Action (DRC/LVS) and Docs Action. Common failures: wrong PDK variable, DRC errors. Our config correctly targets gf180mcuD.

**GF180MCU timing:** 25-40 MHz achievable for decoder-class designs. Our 50 MHz target is aggressive — may need clock_hz adjustment after hardening. OpenLane handles hold repair automatically.

**Bounded formal verification:** DVCon 2025 paper confirms bounded proofs acceptable for sign-off when bounds exceed design state depth. Our BMC depth=30 for FSM (max path=12 cycles) satisfies this criterion.

**MXDOTP/Vmxdotp (2025-2026):** RISC-V ISA extensions for MX dot products. Defines FP9 (E5M3) intermediate format and two-level exponent sharing — potential future catalog additions.
